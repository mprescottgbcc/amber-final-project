Kitchi

Lesson Horse

Kitchi is a 15.3hh mustang gelding. Before he came to Crimson Ridge, he was a wild mustang in the ranges of South Dakota. He is our intermediate/advaned riding lesson horse as he is high-spirited. He excels at jumping and loves to please!

Nike

Lesson Horse

Nike is a 15.6 quarter horse/lusitano cross gelding. He is our beginner lesson horse and has a been there done that attidude and is a great confidence builder for those just getting into riding.

Stanley

For half lease: $500 plus 50% of care expenses

Stanley is a 15 year old 16.2hh andalusian gelding. He is eager to work and displays a nice energy that makes him a joy to ride. He loves jumping and has jumped up to 4'3" with ease. He is schooling dressage movements from 2nd and 3rd level and is a perfect all around horse for an ambitious rider.

Chiron

For full lease: $900 plus all care expenses

Chiron is a 12 year old 15.9 hh lippizan gelding. He was imported from Lipizzan Stud Piber in Austria after training for 2 years in the Spanish Riding School, and his sire has competed at grand prix. He is consistent in his dressage work and has big controlled movements. He is extremely intelligent and loves to work. He is best suited for a intermediate or advanced rider due to his high energy.
