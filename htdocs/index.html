Welcome to Crimson Ridge Stables!

We are a spacious 75 acre stable located in Bridlewood, Cantershire. Crimson Ridge was purchased by Brooke and Vanessa Colene back in 2015. Brooke is a successful retired FEI competitor of 20 years and has started and trained many horses to FEI. After retiring, she decided to run Crimson Ridge Stables and train the next generation in showjumping. She owns a Hanoverian gelding named Hero, who has been with her throughout her eventing career and share an unbreakable bond.

Our Mission

At Crimson Ridge Stables, our mission is to build confidence and establish safe, secure and well-rounded equestrians. We are committed to providing quality care for both you and your horse. Explore our services, meet our horses, and see the facilities that make us a great choice for your all equestrian needs.
